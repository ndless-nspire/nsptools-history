====================================
Advanced Tetris for TI-Nspire/Ndless
====================================

This is a quick and dirty port of GDO's Advanced Tetris built for the TI-Nspire calculator series
with Ndless and nSDL.

Ndless r825 is required: http://ndlessly.wordpress.com/

Many thanks to GDO for the original version: http://www.gd-experience.eu/index.php?viewPost=50
